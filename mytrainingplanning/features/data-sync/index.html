Data Sync from and to Strava via Intervals.icu

Automated sync starting from Strava as my data hub

Once an activity got uploaded to Strava it gets synced to #mytrainingplaning in an automated fashion. It takes an extra route through Interval.icu nowerdays and gets finally all activities to my database. I don´t have to do nothing more than just ride, swim or run, record it and get it uploaded to Strava.

Automated renaming at Strava

It will also automatically, if wanted and configured, rename all my activities at Strava from the current season, update activity names and/or descriptions back as they fit to #mytrainingplanning planned sessions. And even this part can be maintained at any point in time upfront or later because deltas being identified and corrected with the next automation cycle.

That´s what I call: UX!

Pre-Reqs

There are just 2+1 pre-reqs required:
(1) A Google Account to access Google Sheets
(2) Strava
(3) make.com, a cloud automation tool to sync meta data back and forth between Strava and Google Sheets
(4) Intervals.icu, which is the main data provider via APIs to #mytrainingplanning

*Over the time I digged deeper, deeper and deeper in those toolings and cooked fancy blueprints, a recipes, that all the magic happens. Awesome stuff.
