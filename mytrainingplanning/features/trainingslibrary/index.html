Traininglibrary

Maximum level of flexibility

My training library is made to start super simple, to grown steady over the time with a maximum level of flexibility for all my bike, run or strength training sessions. The creamy cherry on the lemon cake is the individual usage of all my Pacing&Racing parameters like heartrate, pace or power thresholds which are being adjusted to each need of a dedicated training session.

Some key assets:

  • When building up such an individual training library categorization become a central component as reports being generated with this meta information.
  • The assignment to seasonal periods are also nice helpers when planning training sessions week in and week out.
  • Maintaining optional Garmin Training IDs, for the case when I have Garmin trainings at hand, help to quickly get a session to my sport gadgets.

Some Examples:

  • The bike split for a 70.3 race simulation is better done in race-pace-manner following a run off the bike.
  • A longer bike FatMax session to become more efficent towards race days looks completely different and using totally different pacing parameter.
