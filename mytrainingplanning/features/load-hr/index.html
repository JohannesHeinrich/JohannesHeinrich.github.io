Heartrate Load

Heartrate(s)

  • The top line is nothing more than the most recent maximum measured HR in the last 30 days.
  • The dark red line reflects a rolling HR maximum average per training session from the last 30 days including a predition based on already planned training sessions. The dark red line is surfaced with a historical and upcoming trend zone to reflect maximum HR stress.
  • The red line reflects a rolling HR average per training session from the last 30 days including a prediction based on already planned training sessions. The red line is surfaced with a historical and upcoming trend zone to reflect average HR stress.
  • The bottom section is the HR load on daily basis supported with a trendline. Again, here are trained and planned training sessions considered.
