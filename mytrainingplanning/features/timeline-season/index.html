Timeline Season

This is the seasonal view.

Beside the planning this is potentially the most important diagram which calculates and displays the most important KPIs which are required for #mytrainingplanning.

Fitness Charts (1st/third)

The classical part of the chart:

  • Red line: ATL “Acute Training Load assumes fatigue from the most recent training load”.
  • Blue line: CTL “Chronic Training Load reflects fitness which is built over a longer period of time”.

The predictable part of the chart:

  • The ATL is embedded in past and upcoming training load windows indicating recovering, maintaining, progressing, stimulating or overtraining. The parameters behind can also be individually configured.
  • The CTL is accentuated with a potential peak prediction light-blue line, which shall help to get in form at the best point in time of the season.

Form Charts (2nd/third)

The classical part of the chart:

  • Neon green line: TSB “Training Stress Balance predicts form to become race day ready”.

The predictable part of the chart:

  • Light purple area: Transitioning for tapering from recovering to peak performance.
  • Dark purple area: Reaching individual peak performance.
  • Light green area: Transitioning during training towards progressing and stimulating.
  • Dark green area: Reaching best stimulating and training load.

Training Load (3rd/third)

  • Daily Training Load: Shows the daily training load in a simple block chart.
  • Accentuated with a smart line chart in the back to indicate past and upcoming load.
