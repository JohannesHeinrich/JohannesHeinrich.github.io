Events

1-12-365 a sheer massiv amount of events should, could and will happen. It has to be structured, otherwise I turn lost in circles. There are some #mytrainingplanning places where different events being maintained to be reused over and over based on needs.

Google Calendar Integration for Day Events

This one is actually a nice und hidden integration into my Google Calendar. All day events I maintain and optionally hashtag in my Google Calendar are showing up automatically in many diagrams and at central planning views.

Various other events

And there are many other additional events like Day Intensities, Day Notes, Week Notes, Weekly Focus and the Season. Around each type is some backend logic in place to display in diagrams or at places where each brings value.
